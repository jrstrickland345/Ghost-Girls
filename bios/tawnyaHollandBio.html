Tawnya Holland


Tawnya is a native of Las Vegas, Nevada. From the time she was very young, she has been clairvoyant, having perceptual dreams and having premonitions. As she got older, she became even more in tune with her energy powers by honing her healing talents. This got her involved with the world of 'Reki' massage. A spiritual technique of healing and restoring health through energy. While also exploring the performing arts, she was drawn to the spirit world with spirits contacting her. She became so connected with spirit friends, that no matter where she is or what she is doing, spirits are there with her.
