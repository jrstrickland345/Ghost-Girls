Crystle Lightning


Crystle Lightning is everything that her given name implies: a clear bolt of penetrating energy. Born in Alberta, Canada of plains Cree American Indian descent. When she was in her early teens, she had her most important spiritual experience,which linked with her with her native American heritage. From this point, Crystle has been in tune with her spiritual gifts. As she grew, she attended the prestigious Fashion Institute of Design & amp; Marketing in Los Angeles, then to the Beverly Hills Playhouse. Being in the entertainment industry, she has appeared in films,such as: LEGEND OF BIGFOOT, MYSTERY OF NAVAJO MESA, and ESCAPE FROM FIRE LAKE. Crystle is now known being a top DJ in the LA club scene, and just finished her first music album.
