Janet Spitzer


Janet was raised in Lviv, Ukraine, in the foothills of the Carpathian mountains only 50 miles from the old country of 'Transylvania'. Since she was a child she has had deep spiritual and clairvoyant roots of her native land and her connection with the spiritual world. Her mentality has been shaped by several progressive schools of thought, and in particular teachings of Osho, Carl Young, Sergei Gurdjie, and Kabbalah, which allowed her to form her own position in life, and a unique perspective on reality. Being a fan of great acting teacher as Richard Boleslavsky and Constantin Stanislavsky, she improved her vision of being an acting teacher in America.
